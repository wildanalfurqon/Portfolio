Plug And Find

Mixed Media Installation

Four Channel Video, 15” LED Monitor, Speaker, Cable, VGA Splitter, MIDI Key, Akai Mono Jumper, Microcomputer, Custom Video Channel Mixer and Mechanical System.
Various Dimension / 2023

Through public participation, this work provides a space for reflection and dialogue that can enhance awareness of the social complexities often obscured as trivial matters. By offering a deeper and critical perspective on the current relevant social and political conditions, this piece also encourages the emergence of positive change and new thinking within a broader context. relevant social and political conditions, this work also pushes for the creation of positive change and new thinking in a broader context.
